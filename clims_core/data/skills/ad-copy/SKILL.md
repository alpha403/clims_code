Research and write a complete set of high-converting ad creatives for the following:

**Brief:** $ARGUMENTS

If the brief is missing details, ask for:
- **Brand:** name, niche, tone
- **Offer:** what exactly is being advertised? Price point?
- **Target audience:** who sees this ad? Cold / warm / retargeting?
- **Platform:** Meta (FB/IG) / Google Search / Google Display / TikTok / LinkedIn / YouTube
- **Objective:** awareness / traffic / leads / conversions / calls
- **Budget tier:** small (<$50/day) / medium ($50-500/day) / large (>$500/day)
- **Existing creatives:** what has been tested before? What worked / failed?

---

## PHASE 1 — RESEARCH (run all 4 tracks in parallel)

### Track A — Audience & Awareness Research
- At what awareness level is the cold audience? (Unaware / Problem-aware / Solution-aware / Product-aware)
- What is their primary desire and primary fear around this offer?
- What exact words do they use to describe their problem? (Search Reddit, Quora, reviews in this niche)
- What beliefs does the audience hold that are WRONG and blocking them from buying? (these become ad angles)
- What does this audience scroll past vs stop on?

### Track B — Competitor Ad Research
- Search the Meta Ads Library for the top 5-10 competitors in this niche
- Fetch their active ads — note: hook (first 3 words), format (UGC / static / video / carousel), offer structure, CTA, visual style
- Which ads have been running the longest? (Long-running = profitable)
- What angle is EVERYONE using? (avoid it — find the gap)
- What pain points or desires are completely ABSENT from competitor ads? (this is the opportunity)

### Track C — Platform-Specific Creative Research
- What ad formats and aspect ratios perform best on the target platform right now?
- What video length converts best for this objective and platform?
- What does the top-of-feed vs story vs reel ad experience look like for this niche?
- Fetch current platform best-practice guides (Meta Business, Google Ads Help, TikTok for Business)
- What are the current CPM/CPC benchmarks for this industry? (sets expectations)

### Track D — Hook & Copy Research
- Fetch swipe files and breakdowns of high-performing ads in adjacent niches
- What are the top 5 proven hook formulas for this audience type?
- What offer framing converts best: free trial / money-back guarantee / limited time / social proof / transformation promise?
- What is the ONE emotion the ad must trigger to get a click from this specific audience?

---

## PHASE 2 — ANGLES

Based on research, define 5 distinct creative angles (each angle = a different emotional entry point into the same offer):

1. **Pain angle** — lead with the problem they're suffering right now
2. **Aspiration angle** — lead with the life/outcome they want
3. **Social proof angle** — lead with a result someone else got
4. **Curiosity angle** — open a loop they must click to close
5. **Contrarian angle** — challenge a common belief in the niche

---

## PHASE 3 — WRITE THE AD CREATIVES

For each of the 5 angles, write a complete ad unit:

### Meta (FB/IG) Ad
- **Hook** (first 3 words of primary text — these appear before "See more"): pattern interrupt, bold, platform-native
- **Primary text** (125 chars before cut-off, 500 chars total): hook → problem/desire → solution tease → CTA
- **Headline** (27 chars max, bold below the image): outcome-focused
- **Description** (optional, under the headline): handle one objection or add urgency
- **CTA button:** choose from platform options (Learn More / Sign Up / Get Quote / Book Now / Shop Now)
- **Visual direction:** describe the image or video in enough detail to brief a designer or AI tool ("Split screen: left = stressed nurse at desk / right = nurse smiling outside with passport")

### Google Search Ad (if requested)
- 3 x headlines (30 chars each): keyword-containing, benefit-led, CTA
- 2 x descriptions (90 chars each): expand benefits, handle objection, include CTA
- Display URL path (2 x 15 chars)

### TikTok / Reel Ad (if requested)
- Script: 15-30 second spoken script, hook in first 2 seconds, pattern interrupt visual direction, CTA
- On-screen text overlay suggestions
- Suggested sound/music mood

---

## PHASE 4 — RETARGETING VARIANTS

Write 2 shorter retargeting ads (for warm audiences who have already seen the brand):
- Skip the awareness hook — go straight to objection-handling or urgency
- Reference what they've already seen ("You looked at X — here's why now is the time")
- Stronger CTA and offer (free consult, limited spots, bonus)

---

## PHASE 5 — TESTING PLAN

Deliver a structured A/B test roadmap:

**Week 1 — Hook test:** run 3 hooks against the same body copy. Budget split evenly. Kill losers at 1,000 impressions per ad.

**Week 2 — Angle test:** take the winning hook, test it across 3 different angles. Same visual.

**Week 3 — Offer test:** take the winning angle, test 2 offer framings (e.g. "free call" vs "free guide").

**Week 4 — Scale:** take the winner and increase budget 20% every 2 days while CPA stays within target.

**Metrics to watch:** CTR (benchmark for this niche from research), CPC, CPL/CPA, ROAS. Flag if CTR >3% but CPA is high (landing page problem, not ad problem).

---

## SELF-CHECK (before delivering)

- [ ] Each angle is genuinely distinct — not just the same copy reworded
- [ ] Every hook works in the first 3 words — no warm-up
- [ ] Primary text respects the 125-char before-fold cut-off
- [ ] Headlines are within character limits for each platform
- [ ] Visual directions are specific enough to brief without a call
- [ ] Retargeting ads are clearly different from cold ads
- [ ] No placeholder copy — every ad is ready to upload
- [ ] Testing plan is specific with clear kill criteria
