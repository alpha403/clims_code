Write high-converting, platform-optimised social media captions for the following:

**Brief:** $ARGUMENTS

If the brief is missing details, ask for:
- **Brand:** name, voice/tone, colors, niche
- **Platform(s):** Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, Twitter/X (or all)
- **Post type:** educational, promotional, social proof, story, behind-the-scenes, CTA, announcement
- **Asset:** what image/video will accompany this caption?
- **Goal:** awareness, engagement, link clicks, DMs, sales

## PHASE 1 — RESEARCH (run all 3 tracks in parallel)

### Track A — Audience & Platform Intelligence
- Who is the exact target audience? Demographics, language, pain points, aspirations
- What content format performs best for this audience on each platform right now?
- What posting time and frequency wins for this niche? (fetch current platform algorithm guides)
- Fetch 5-10 top-performing posts in this niche (search relevant hashtags + competitor accounts) — note hook structure, caption length, CTA type, engagement patterns

### Track B — Hook Research
- Fetch and read swipe files, copywriting resources, and viral post breakdowns for this vertical
- What are the top 5 hook formulas that stop the scroll in this niche right now?
- What emotional triggers work for this audience (fear, aspiration, curiosity, controversy, relatability)?
- What single word or phrase do the best-performing posts in this niche lead with?

### Track C — Hashtag & SEO Research
- Research the optimal hashtag strategy for each platform (Instagram: 3-5 niche tags; TikTok: 3 broad + 2 niche; LinkedIn: 3 professional tags)
- What are the primary SEO keywords for this topic on each platform?
- Which hashtags are oversaturated vs underserved in this niche?

## PHASE 2 — WRITE THE CAPTIONS

Write one complete caption per platform requested. Each caption must be ready to copy-paste — no placeholders, no [INSERT X HERE].

### Instagram Caption
- **Hook** (line 1, stands alone before "more"): single sentence, pattern interrupt, speaks directly to the audience's desire or fear — under 125 characters
- **Body**: 3-5 short paragraphs, each under 3 lines. Use line breaks generously. Include the core message, a relatable moment or stat, and one surprising angle
- **CTA**: direct, specific, low-friction ("Comment X below", "Save this for later", "Share with someone who needs this", "Link in bio")
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 niche-relevant tags on a new line after the caption
- **Emoji**: use purposefully — 1-2 per paragraph max, only where they add meaning

### Facebook Caption
- Hook is the full first sentence (no cut-off) — Facebook shows more before the fold
- Slightly longer body than Instagram — storytelling and value work well here
- Strong CTA that drives comments or shares (Facebook algorithm rewards these)
- 2-3 hashtags max

### LinkedIn Caption
- Professional but human tone — personal insight or experience opening works best
- Hook: a bold statement, contrarian take, or specific outcome
- Body: structured with line breaks, no walls of text, can use bullet points
- End with a question to drive comments
- 3 relevant professional hashtags

### TikTok Caption
- Very short (under 150 chars) — the video does the work
- Hook keyword first (algorithm reads the caption)
- 3-5 hashtags mixing broad (#fyp, #viral) and niche tags
- CTA if relevant ("Follow for more", "Comment X")

### Twitter/X Caption (if requested)
- Under 280 characters for the hook tweet
- Thread format if the topic warrants depth (number each tweet 1/, 2/, etc.)
- No hashtags unless highly specific — they hurt reach on X now

## PHASE 3 — VARIATIONS

For the primary platform, write 3 additional hook variations (first line only) so the user can A/B test:
1. **Curiosity hook** — tease the answer without giving it
2. **Pain hook** — name the exact frustration the audience feels
3. **Social proof hook** — open with a result, number, or outcome

---

## PHASE 4 — POSTING NOTES

After the captions, add a brief posting guide:
- Best day + time to post on each platform for this niche (from research)
- What to do in the first 30 minutes after posting (respond to comments, engage with similar content)
- One metric to watch (saves on IG, shares on FB, reposts on X, etc.)
- If the post underperforms: one quick tweak to try (change the hook, boost as an ad, repost at a different time)

## SELF-CHECK (before delivering)

- [ ] Every caption has a hook that works standalone — no warm-up, no intro
- [ ] No caption starts with "I" or the brand name (algorithm penalty + weak hook)
- [ ] CTAs are specific and single — one ask per caption
- [ ] No Lorem Ipsum or placeholder copy anywhere
- [ ] Hashtags are researched and relevant — not generic (#love, #instagood)
- [ ] Tone matches the brand voice from the brief
- [ ] Each caption is formatted for its platform (line breaks, emoji use, length)
