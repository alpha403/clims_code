Research and produce a complete content plan for the following:

**Brief:** $ARGUMENTS

If the brief is missing details, ask for:
- **Brand:** name, niche, tone, core offer
- **Platforms:** Instagram / Facebook / LinkedIn / TikTok / YouTube / blog / email (or all active)
- **Timeframe:** 1 week / 2 weeks / 1 month / 3 months (default: 1 month)
- **Posting frequency per platform:** (default: research recommends)
- **Content pillars already defined?** Or should research define them?
- **Goal this period:** awareness / follower growth / lead generation / sales

---

## PHASE 1 — DEEP RESEARCH (run all 4 tracks in parallel)

### Track A — Audience & Niche Research
- Who is the audience? What are their top 5 content consumption habits?
- What topics in this niche get the most engagement? (search hashtags, Reddit, YouTube comment sections, Facebook groups)
- What questions does this audience keep asking? (Answer The Public, Quora, Reddit — extract the EXACT phrasing)
- What content formats does this audience prefer: educational, entertainment, inspiration, behind-the-scenes, social proof, controversy?
- What triggers them to follow, save, share, or comment?

### Track B — Platform Algorithm & Trend Research
- Fetch current algorithm best-practice guides for each platform in the brief
- What posting frequency and timing is optimal for each platform right now for this niche?
- What content format is the algorithm currently boosting on each platform (reels, carousels, text posts, long-form video)?
- What trending sounds, challenges, or formats are blowing up in this niche right now?
- What hashtag and keyword strategy is working on each platform?

### Track C — Competitor Content Audit
- Identify the top 5 competitors or adjacent creators in this niche
- For each: fetch their profile, note their posting frequency, top content formats, engagement rate on recent posts, recurring content series
- What content gaps are they missing that this brand could own?
- What is their weakest content pillar? (opportunity to dominate)
- What is their single highest-performing content type? (benchmark to beat)

### Track D — Seasonal & Event Calendar Research
- What industry events, holidays, or cultural moments are relevant to this niche in the timeframe?
- What product launches, announcements, or business events is the brand running?
- Any awareness days, trending topics, or seasonal angles that should anchor the calendar?

---

## PHASE 2 — CONTENT PILLARS

Based on research, define 4-5 content pillars (categories that every post belongs to):

| Pillar | % of content | Purpose | Example formats |
|---|---|---|---|
| Educate | 30% | Build authority + trust | How-to, tips, myth-busting, explainers |
| Inspire | 20% | Emotional connection | Success stories, transformations, quotes |
| Entertain | 20% | Reach + shareability | Behind-the-scenes, relatable humour, trends |
| Convert | 20% | Direct revenue | Offer posts, CTAs, testimonials, promos |
| Engage | 10% | Community + algorithm | Questions, polls, challenges, user content |

Adjust percentages to fit the brand's current goal (more Convert posts if sales-focused, more Educate if building authority).

## PHASE 3 — BUILD THE CALENDAR

Produce a full content calendar for the requested timeframe as a structured table:

| Date | Day | Platform | Pillar | Format | Topic / Hook | CTA | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2026-07-07 | Mon | Instagram | Educate | Reel | "3 things nurses don't know about the German Opportunity Card" | Comment "Germany" | Pair with infographic b-roll |
| 2026-07-07 | Mon | Facebook | Educate | Post + image | Same topic, longer caption | Link in bio | Repurpose IG caption |
| 2026-07-08 | Tue | LinkedIn | Inspire | Text post | Client success story: nurse in Germany | DM me | Tag client if permitted |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

**Repurposing column:** note where one piece of content can be repurposed across platforms to reduce production load (e.g. a Reel script becomes a LinkedIn post becomes an email).

---

## PHASE 4 — CONTENT BRIEFS

For the first week (or first 5 posts if <1 week plan), write full content briefs — not just topics, but the actual hook, angle, and talking points:

For each post:
- **Hook** (exact first line or first 3 words of the video)
- **Key points** (3 bullet points of what to cover)
- **CTA** (exact words)
- **Visual direction** (what to film/design)
- **Hashtags** (pre-researched)

---

## PHASE 5 — CONTENT PRODUCTION QUEUE

After the calendar, produce a production schedule:

**What needs to be produced this week:**
- X Reels → trigger `/reel` skill for each
- X static graphics → trigger `/post-image` skill for each
- X captions → trigger `/caption` skill for each
- X email → trigger `/email` skill

**Batch recording suggestions:** group all talking-head filming into one session, group all graphic design into one session.

**Tools needed:** list any external tools, accounts, or assets needed to execute this calendar.

---

## SELF-CHECK (before delivering)

- [ ] Every post belongs to a defined pillar
- [ ] No two consecutive posts are the same format on the same platform
- [ ] At least one post per week directly supports the primary business goal
- [ ] Seasonal/event dates are correctly incorporated
- [ ] Repurposing opportunities are flagged (saves production time)
- [ ] First week has full content briefs, not just topics
- [ ] Calendar is realistic for the team size and production capacity
